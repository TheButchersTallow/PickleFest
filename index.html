🥒 About Pickle Fest TXK 🥒

WHO

Founder: Monica Beeman
Pickle Fest Texarkana is a community-driven festival created to bring people together through fun, food, and a whole lot of pickle-themed excitement. Founded by Monica Beeman, who loves celebrating small businesses, families, and regional culture, Pickle Fest is powered by volunteers, sponsors, vendors, and thousands of attendees who make this event unforgettable each year.

WHAT

Pickle Fest Texarkana is a one-day, high-energy festival packed with entertainment, shopping, competitions, and unique experiences you won't find anywhere else. From pickle-inspired foods and specialty retail vendors to live music, contests, games, and family activities, there's something for everyone.

Festival highlights include:
• Pickle-inspired food and drinks
• Retail and artisan vendors
• Live entertainment and music
• Family-friendly activities
• Competitions, contests, and special events
• 5K race and car show (2026 and beyond!)

WHEN

📅 Saturday, October 10, 2026 • 10AM-5PM
🕘 Full-day festival event (schedule released closer to festival date)

WHERE

📍 Downtown Texarkana — Front Street Festival Plaza
Located in the heart of downtown, making it easy to explore local shops, restaurants, and attractions while enjoying the festival.

WHY

Pickle Fest Texarkana was created to:
• Support small businesses and local vendors
• Create a unique regional event for people to travel for
• Bring families, friends, and communities together
• Boost local tourism and economic growth
• Celebrate fun, creativity, and (of course) pickles

What started as a bold idea quickly became one of the region's most talked-about community events, and we're just getting started.

HOW
